Ethical Use of Generative AI

1. Transparency

Disclose AI-generated content to build trust and prevent misinformation.

2. Privacy Protection

Use AI tools carefully; avoid entering sensitive data unless secure.

3. Avoiding Plagiarism

Use AI as a draft or guide, not the final submission.

4. Bias Awareness

Critically review AI outputs to avoid reinforcing biases.

5. Accountability

Humans remain responsible for verifying facts and ethical use.
