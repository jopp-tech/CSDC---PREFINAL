Understanding Generative AI Through Real-Life Scenarios

Generative AI is like a versatile assistant that can help generate text, images, and even videos, but it relies on human oversight. Its outputs are based on patterns in the data it was trained on. Using GenAI responsibly requires checking its outputs, being aware of biases, and validating accuracy.

I believe GenAI is a powerful tool when used correctly. It can inspire creativity and save time, but humans must remain in control. Even with advanced AI, critical thinking and editing are essential.
